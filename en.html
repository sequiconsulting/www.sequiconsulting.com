Credentials & Affiliations

A professional framework that ensures transparency, methodology, and continuous development.

UniExportManager

Active member of UniExportManager, the Italian professional association of Export Managers and International Business Specialists. UniExportManager is recognised by the Italian Ministry of Economic Development and operates under Law 4/2013, which regulates non‑regulated professions and guarantees the competence, continuous training, and ethical conduct of its members.

Law 4/2013 Framework

Italian Law 4/2013 regulates non‑organised professions, protecting consumers by requiring certified professionals to meet standards of competence, transparency, continuous professional development, and ethical conduct.

As a qualified professional under this law and a member of a Law 4/2013‑recognised association (UniExportManager), my practice is subject to independent quality oversight.

Where I go deeper

Four pillars of expertise. Each one earned through years of hands‑on work.

Export Strategy & Business Internationalisation

Expanding abroad is not a sales activity — it is a strategic decision that shapes your company for years. I help you answer the hard questions: where to establish a branch or subsidiary, why one jurisdiction over another, and how the local operating environment will affect your business — whether you are selling, buying, or operating between third countries.

My analysis covers the regulatory framework, tax and corporate law, labour market, infrastructure, supply‑chain proximity, cultural fit, and political stability of each candidate country. I produce structured comparisons — pros, cons, risks, and timelines — so you can make an informed choice. As a UniExportManager member, I apply a rigorous methodology, not gut feeling. I do not act as a commercial agent: I design the international architecture of your company.

Regulatory & Customs Compliance

The regulatory landscape is shifting faster than most businesses can track. From CE marking to customs classification, from dual‑use restrictions to export licensing, from product environmental regulations (REACH, RoHS, WEEE) to EPR and PFAS obligations — I have spent years helping manufacturers, traders, and service companies stay compliant and avoid penalties.

I interpret regulations not as obstacles but as design constraints that shape better products and stronger market positions. My compliance work covers: product compliance for EU and non‑EU markets, CE marking, customs classification (HS codes), preferential and non‑preferential origin, AEO authorisations, dual‑use restrictions, import/export licensing, and customs documentation management — for inbound, outbound, and third‑country operations.

Custom Software Development

I have been writing code since before the web existed. Today I build custom software for companies that want to automate processes, integrate disparate systems, modernise legacy applications, or develop web platforms from scratch — not as a technology exercise, but as a lever to reduce costs, eliminate errors, and free your people for higher‑value work.

From ERP customisation to document management systems, from REST API design to progressive web applications, from legacy system migration to cloud‑native solutions: I deliver working systems, not slide decks. Every line of code I write is built to be maintained, extended, and handed over — never a black box.

Artificial Intelligence for Business Processes

AI is not a gadget — it is a tool that, applied with judgement, transforms business processes. I assess your organisation's AI readiness, identify the workflows that genuinely benefit from intelligent automation, and integrate LLM components into your systems — from document processing to internal chatbots, from automatic classification pipelines to predictive dashboards.

My approach always starts with the process, not the technology: first I understand what is slowing you down, then I choose whether and which AI component can make a difference. From rapid prototyping to production deployment, I guide you through every stage — with attention to data governance, security, and the long‑term sustainability of the solution.
